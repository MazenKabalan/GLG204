CNAM HISTORY

July 3, 1968: Decree of the Lebanese Republic (No. 10434) on "Constitution of an Institute at the Lebanese University - Institute of Applied and Economic Sciences which becomes in 2008 Higher Institute of Applied and Economic Sciences (ISSAE - Cnam Lebanon).

Article 3 of this decree: The present institute will operate according to a special agreement between:
- Lebanese University
- The National Conservatory of Arts and Crafts, Paris
- The Lebanese Association for Scientific, Technical and Economic Education.
It will also have to be approved by the Minister of National Education and Fine Arts.

13 April 1973: Settlement Agreement between the Lebanese University, the Lebanese Association for Scientific Technical and Economic Education and CNAM setting the conditions under which the three institutions collaborate in the management of ISSAE (Institute Superior of Applied and Economic Sciences).

In 2011, nearly 4,000 auditors received instruction partly in French at ISSAE centers in BEIRUT, BAALBEK, BAAKLINE, TRIPOLI, BIKFAYA and GHAZA.

November 2011: Inauguration of the NAHR IBRAHIM center located in an industrial area.
